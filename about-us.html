Overview

Eldoret Grains Limited (EGL DOLA) has established itself as a dynamic and the most trusted supplier in the local consumer foods industry. We have mastered the science and art of milling the finest flour products and take pride on offering customers guaranteed quality and consistency.

Committed to nourishing the nation, Eldoret Grains Limited mills are set to ensure provision of wholesome and nutritious flour, fortified with vitamins and minerals for the benefit of the whole family unit and the nation.

Eldoret Grains Limited mills has developed a range of flour products that cater to diverse dietary preferences and needs and are available in a variety of volumes and packaging formats which have gradually won the hearts of customers and consumers across the Rift and many other various regions across the country and beyond our Kenyan borders.

History

Eldoret Grains Limited mills began its journey in 1998 and after successfully completing the construction of its milling facility company commenced commercial operations in 2008 and introduced its very first product, Premium Maize Flour and Home-Baking Flour in 2Kg, 1Kg and 500grams packets to the market. The company has since then enjoyed substantial growth over the years, securing both consumer loyalty and a rapidly increasing brand presence across the rift and other regions of the country.

EGL is located at the home of champions, Eldoret, Iten road near Kipchoge Stadium. The mill is installed with state of art technology equipment and with its most modern roller mills in the region, it has the capacity of 600 ton of Maize and 600 ton of wheat per day.

In 2018, the company rebranded its logo and packaging and expanded its retail range to include Atta- Mark 1, Chapati, Self- raising, Mandazi and Superior Maize Meal (EGL Dola Gold) flour in 2Kg and 1 Kg packets. We renewed our focus on strategic expansion and operational efficiency, which paved the way for the company’s continued growth.
